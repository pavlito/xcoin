About iPrime

iPrime is a next generation brokerage company. Our team has more than a decade of experience providing Forex brokerage services and proprietary trading. Our instruments include Forex, Precious Metals, Commodities, Indices and Futures.

Proprietary trading requires top-edge technologies. iPrime developed proprietary ECN platform to automatically route orders between multiple tier-1 liquidity providers. Nowadays what we developed for ourselves is available for retail traders. Technical team automatically monitors the execution speed and available liquidity. Our liquidity is one of the best in the market. Make sure even large orders will be filled in millisecond time

iPrime supports only "market execution" mode, the priority is fastest execution without re-quotes. There could be slippage involved when final execution price differs from original order price, but we always forward full positive (price benefit) and negative slippage back to our clients. ECN system does not restrict pending orders so you can place limit and take profit orders within the spread and scalp the market.
